Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

We recommend booking online to guarantee your preferred groomer and time — especially for full boutique experiences and weekends.

Can I request a specific groomer?

Absolutely. Choose "Any available groomer" for the fastest slot, or pick a named groomer during booking if you have a preference.

What if I need to cancel or change my time?

Use "Manage booking" in the top menu with your reference code and email to cancel any time before your appointment.

Is my dog safe with you?

Yes — all groomers are fully insured, trained in fear-free handling, and every dog is supervised throughout their visit.

How early should I arrive?

Please arrive 5 minutes before your slot with your dog on a secure lead. Running more than 10 minutes late may mean your appointment needs to be rescheduled.
